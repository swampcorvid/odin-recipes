Cheesy Bacon Chicken with Mustard Sauce


Source: iamhomesteader.com

Description

Cheesy Bacon Chicken with Mustard Sauce is chicken breasts marinated in honey mustard sauce and baked topped with bacon and cheese. It is based on the Alice Springs Chicken on the menu at Outback Steakhouse restaurants but without the mushrooms.

Ingredients:

Honey Mustard Marinade

  • 1/2 cup (124.5 g) yellow mustard
  • 1/2 cup (170 g) honey
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper

Chicken

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 8 slices bacon, cooked
  • 1 cup (113 g) Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup (113 g) mild cheddar cheese, shredded
  • parsley, for garnish
